Q1 Company philosophy

The concept of "food as medicine" has existed since ancient times, and it implies the use of certain medicinal herbs as ingredients in cuisine or various food products. For example, adding ingredients like lily bulbs, tremella mushrooms, or goji berries to dishes not only enhances their flavor and nutritional value but also serves to nourish the body. We have dedicated the past decade to exploring the essence of this "food as medicine" philosophy, focusing on the research of plant extracts. Our goal is to improve overall health through dietary therapy. By incorporating the principles of Traditional Chinese Medicine's Five Elements theory, we aim to help individuals maintain their well-being and balance their body's energy while enjoying their meals. Most importantly, we firmly believe that a change of the "mind" can ignite the dormant flame of hope. At the core of this concept is the idea that through the synergy of food and medicine, we can harness the healing power of nature in our diet to enhance physical health. Simultaneously, it is crucial to emphasize inner tranquility and positive thinking, as they can have profound effects on the body.

Brand story

Establisher

「I found the possibility of rebirth in life.」

Six years ago, we were a small-scale animal food research and development, plant extraction, and processing export factory located in the Tainan Xinying Industrial Zone. Despite the relatively modest size of our factory, our mission was monumental. We dedicated ourselves to assisting local farmers in overcoming difficulties in animal husbandry while simultaneously increasing their average yield. Though we were small like sparrows, our factory possessed all the necessary facilities and professional expertise to fulfill this mission. However, as our business gradually stabilized and thrived, an old ailment stemming from a car accident 30 years ago cast a shadow over my daily life, subjecting me to pain and feelings of despair. Fortunately, the unwavering support and assistance from family and friends saw me through the toughest times. Despite surviving, the accident left behind numerous lingering effects, significantly impacting my life. In order to rediscover myself, relentless efforts were invested. Engaging in continuous plant extraction research day and night, I eventually succeeded in extracting "isothiocyanate" from horseradish, the essence of pure horseradish extract. This miraculous plant extract not only restored me to a normal life but also significantly rejuvenated my appearance. This is not just a story of technological breakthrough but also a miracle of life.

「Our Future」

We will continue to leverage our unique extraction technology to uncover the essence of natural plants, allowing more people to appreciate the blessings of nature.
