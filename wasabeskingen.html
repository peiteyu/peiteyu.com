Wasabi

Japan, the land known as "green gold".
A precious plant was born - Wasabi.

Many mustard products on the market are not all-natural; they are often made from an ingredient called horseradish. Horseradish has a lower economic value than real Wasabi and is relatively cheap. So while they may have some spicy flavor, they can't completely replace the unique flavor and nutritional value of pure wasabi. Real wasabi is a plant that grows in cold mountains and requires a special environment to cultivate. Its unique flavor and spiciness are the result of a long period of evolution and adaptation to the mountain environment, and are difficult to completely imitate with other ingredients.

Growth environment

Because its leaves resemble those of the Malvaceae family and grow on high mountains, it is called "wasabi".
If it is cultivated at high altitude, it needs to be grown in a cool, cool place. In addition, it needs to maintain an environment with flowing water to grow well.

Shape

Native to China and Japan, it is known as "Eutrema tenue".
It is a cruciferous herbaceous plant. Its underground rhizome is slender and strong, with traces of fallen petioles. The leaves are clustered and the long leaves are heart-shaped.

Exclusive extraction, "Extracting the Most Essential Part"

The special component hidden in wasabi, namely isothiocyanates, is where the miracle lies. Surprisingly, this mysterious essence is most abundant in the "rhizome" of wasabi.Therefore, in extracting the essence of wasabi,we insist on not taking leaves, petioles, or flowers, focusing solely on the pure extraction of the "rhizome"to ensure you experience the purest miracle of wasabi.
