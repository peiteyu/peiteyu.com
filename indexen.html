「From planting to production, quality control is strictly controlled.」

Peiteyu International Co., Ltd., which originated from the extraction of natural foods, has been steadily expanding its footprint. From brands and distribution channels to biotech foods, we have integrated the entire marketing market. To ensure stable production, our company manages all aspects, including cultivation, production, extraction, and finished products, in-house, creating a seamless, one-stop strategic model for high-quality production. We believe that in the international export market, it is not feasible to compete solely based on quantity; instead, quality becomes the key factor. In today's market, consumers prioritize food safety and convenience. Our company's environment, testing, and production quality have all been certified by SGS, ensuring that our products meet the highest standards.

We handle everything from cultivation to production ourselves. Currently, the planting site for Wasabi is located in the central region, near the mountains.
